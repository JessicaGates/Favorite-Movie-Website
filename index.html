Mama Mia!

The Smash Hit Musical Movie based on the songs of ABBA!

MAMA MIA is a movie about a mother, Donna, and daughter, Sophie. Sophie has no idea who her father is and is getting married soon. She found her mothers journal and learns of three possible men her father could be. She invites all three to Greece for her wedding in secret. What could possibly go wrong? Find out in MAMA MIA!
